IPDC Workshops 2022

The 2022 workshop will be hybrid.

Virtual: June 13-16 from 2:30 – 5 PM Central Time

In-person June 20-22 from 9 AM – 5 PM Central Time at Tennessee Tech University, Cookeville, TN.

Participants will have approximately one-two hours of additional offline work each day.

$4000 stipend for each participant paid in two installments ($2500 to defray the cost of attendance, and $1500 for implementation effort)

US citizens, permanent resident, and a person with valid US work permit are eligible for the stipend.
